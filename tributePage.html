The Timeline of Madame Curie life

  • 1867 - Born in Poland.
  • 1881 - Moved to Paris to study at Sorbonne.
  • 1895 - Married Pierre Curie, whcih was when her name became Marie Curie.
  • 1897 - First daughter, Irene, is born.
  • 1902 - Discoverd pure radium while working on some experiments.
  • 1903 - Were awarded a Nobel Prise with her husband, Pierre, for their work with radioactivity.
  • 1904 - Was pormoted to the chief assistant to her husband in his laboratory.
  • 1904 - Pierre began teaching at Sorbonne.
  • 1906 - Became a widow, her husbane, Pierre, was killed in an accident.
  • 1906 - Was the first female professor at Sorbonne.
  • 1911 - Won her secode Nobel Prise on her own for her continued work in radioactivity.
  • 1914 - Used her radioactive research materials to do "x-rays" during WWI.
  • 1922 - Became a member of the French Academy of Medicine.
  • 1922 - Devoted herself to using her knowledge to help people.
  • 1929 - Visited America, and was given a gram of radium as a gift.
  • 1932 -And her sister started a research facility to help fight cancer. It is still opened today and is called the Marie Sklodowska Curie Oncology Center.
  • 1934 - dies at age of 67.
